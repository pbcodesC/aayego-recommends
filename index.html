Lovely runner up

What if you could go back in time to save your favorite idol from dying? That’s exactly what happens to Im Sol, a passionate fan of Ryu Seon-jae, a famous idol who tragically dies. She magically wakes up in 2008, back in her high school days—when Seon-jae was just a student, not yet a star. Armed with her knowledge of the future, Sol makes it her mission to protect Seon-jae from his painful fate and help him live a better life. But things get complicated as romantic feelings bloom and timelines begin to shift. Can she change destiny without losing him all over again?
